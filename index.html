About us

We are Zipties, a team of 7 students from the Heerbeeck College in Best. This season is our Rookie year, where we design, build, and program a robot to complete complicated tasks. Our name, Zipties, fits us well. Just like normal zip ties, we hold things together and never give up, no matter how difficult the challenge! We work as a team, think of smart solutions, and always keep learning. Our team is made up of students of different ages and education levels. We all bring unique skills in engineering, programming, design, and strategy. Together, we learn about robotics, teamwork, and problem-solving.
