Previous seasons

This section covers our experiences and developments across various FTC seasons.

DECODE

During the DECODE season we built a few iterations that eventually led to our final design. Our robot was at first based of the goBILDA starterbot, but thanks to rapid prototyping we were able to develop more-efficient and accurate iterations. We also developed custom 3D-parts in CAD to enhance our robot's performance. Some benefits of this approach included:

  • Faster development time: We were able to quickly test and iterate on our designs, allowing us to identify and fix issues early on.
  • Optimization of tasks: By utilizing 3D printing and such we were able to optimize our robot's performance for specific tasks, such as scoring, navigating the field and even loading artifacts without human intervention.
